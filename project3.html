Project Overview

This project involved designing a fully functional office desk in SolidWorks, complete with drawers, cabinets, and support structures. The work demonstrated proficiency in part modeling, assembly design, and the creation of exploded views, highlighting both technical accuracy and practical functionality in 3D modeling.
