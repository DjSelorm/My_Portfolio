Turning Superstore data into clear business insights through interactive dashboards

Excel Tableau SQL

Built a dashboard highlighting key KPIs and visual trends to track revenue, profit, and customer performance across categories and segments.

Project Overview

This project involved analyzing Superstore sales data from 2015 to 2018 using Tableau. The interactive dashboard highlights annual performance metrics, including profit, revenue, profit ratio, and order quantity. It also breaks down customer performance, monthly profit trends, segment-wise profitability, and category-level contributions, providing a comprehensive view of business performance.

Key Insights

  • Profits increased steadily from 2015 to 2018, with the highest profit observed in 2018.
  • Technology contributed the most profit (~$145K), followed by Office Supplies (~$122K), while Furniture lagged behind (~$18K).
  • Corporate and Consumer segments were more profitable compared to Home Office.
  • Retention and YOY growth were stronger in Technology and Office Supplies than Furniture.
